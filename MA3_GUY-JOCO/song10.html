Bawat Kaluluwa

By: IV of Spades

Return to Homepage
"Bawat Kaluluwa," meaning "Every Soul" in English, is a song by the Filipino band IV of Spades, and it's a track from their album "CLAPCLAPCLAP!". The song's lyrics explore themes of losing one's sense of purpose and being consumed by thoughts and responsibilities, ultimately leading to a feeling of being haunted by one's passion.
Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)
