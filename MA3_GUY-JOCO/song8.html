Dilaw

By: Maki

Return to Homepage
"Dilaw" is an indie alternative track that explores the themes of unconditional love. The music video, co-directed by Jaydee Alberto and Maki, portrays a romantic narrative between two young artists, played by Maki and Maloi of the P-pop girl group Bini.
Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)
