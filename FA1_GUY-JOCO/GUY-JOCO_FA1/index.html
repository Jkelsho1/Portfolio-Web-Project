FF

Fireflies

"Are a revolutionary militia group that opposes FEDRA, the military organization controlling the quarantine zones. Their goal is to restore the American government and fight for democracy, even if it means resorting to violence and political instability. The Fireflies are also actively seeking a cure for the cordyceps infection."

WLF

Washington Liberation Front

"Is a militaristic faction that emerged in Seattle after the outbreak and the establishment of a FEDRA quarantine zone. They initially fought FEDRA for better living conditions but eventually took over, becoming a powerful and authoritarian group, much like the FEDRA they replaced. The WLF, also known as "Wolves," are led by Isaac Dixon and are known for their violent tactics and control over Seattle."

FEDRA

Federal Disaster Response Agency

"Often referred to as the military or army, is a government agency that seized control of the US government and armed forces after the Cordyceps brain infection outbreak. They established quarantine zones (QZs) and became a military dictatorship, imposing martial law."

SCARS

Seraphites

"Are a religious cult, also known as the Seraphites, who emerged in Seattle after a woman, the Prophet, had a vision promoting spiritual redemption. They are characterized by their minimalist, communal lifestyle, rejection of pre-pandemic technology, and belief that the Cordyceps infection is a divine punishment. "

JSC

Jackson Community

"A thriving community of survivors and a town in Wyoming. The Jackson community is a safe haven where survivors, including Joel and Ellie, find refuge after years of hardship."

Random Survivor

Survivor

"Is a human who has not been infected by the Cordyceps brain infection. They are often described as hardened and resourceful, struggling to survive in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by infected humans. Survivors can be found in quarantine zones, independent settlements, or as lone wanderers, and many are hostile towards others."
